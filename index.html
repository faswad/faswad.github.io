Profile

Academic background, research interests, and technical practice

Firas Aswad holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Florida Institute of Technology. He earned his Master of Science degree in Computer Science from the University of Technology, Baghdad, Iraq, in 2005, and his Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Mosul University, Iraq, in 2002.

He is a certified Cisco instructor and obtained an IT Administration certification from TU Berlin in 2010. His main research interests include Network Science and Data Science.

His professional background also includes Linux systems, network operations, automation, technical support, and the practical application of data-driven methods to complex real-world problems.
